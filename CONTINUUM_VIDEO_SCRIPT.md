## **Part 2: Role-Based Identity & Clinical Verification**
**[Visual: Quick montage of the role-selection page, followed by the Doctor signup form showing the 'Medical License' field]**

**Script:**
"Trust is the foundation of Continuum. Our dual-portal architecture begins with a rigorous onboarding process tailored to your role.

For **Patients**, onboarding establishes your **Clinical Identity**. We require essential data like Date of Birth and Gender right from the start. These aren't just profile fields—they are the baseline parameters our AI uses to calculate your Continuum Score and analyze your health memory with clinical precision. Whether you verify via email or mobile OTP, your identity is the key to your secure, synchronized health journey."

## **Part 4: Health Memory (AI Conversations & Clinical Sync)**
**[Visual: Screen recording of a chat session, using a voice log, and then clicking 'Analyze & Sync']**

**Script:**
"The most powerful way to build your medical history is through **Health Memory**. We’ve replaced rigid forms with natural, human conversation. 

Whether you’re typing a quick update or using **Voice Logs** to record a symptom as it happens, our AI Health Buddy is always listening with clinical context. You can even upload photos of lab reports or medication bottles for **Instant Image Analysis**. 

But the real magic happens when you hit **'Analyze & Sync'**. Our engine parses your unstructured conversation, extracts clinical facts, and synchronizes them into a structured health timeline. This ensures that every symptom, medication change, and observation is captured with clinical-grade accuracy without you ever having to fill out a form."

## **Part 5: Symptom Tracker (Anatomical Pattern Intelligence)**
**[Visual: Navigating to the Symptom Tracker, interacting with the Body Heatmap, and showing the 'Hidden Patterns' card]**

**Script:**
"Our **Symptom Tracker** isn't just a log; it’s a physiological diagnostic engine. At its core is the interactive **Body Heatmap**. You can tap on specific anatomical nodes—from the front deltoids to the lumbar region—to visualize exactly where you’re experiencing discomfort.

When you log a symptom, you’re prompted to record its intensity and clinical triggers—like stress, dehydration, or physical activity levels. But where it gets truly intelligent is in the **Pattern Analysis**. Our AI searches your longitudinal data to find correlations you might miss—for example, automatically identifying that your tension headaches are occurring 12 hours after a poor sleep cycle. It turns 'random' pain into 'readable' patterns for you and your doctor."

## **Part 6: Smart Medications & Safety Analysis**
**[Visual: Navigating to Medications, showing the 'Safety Check' header and adding a new medication with the interaction warning appearing]**

**Script:**
"Managing medications can be dangerous without the right oversight. Our **Smart Medications** portal is a dedicated safety layer for your prescriptions. 

When you add a new medication, Continuum doesn't just record it—it performs an instant **AI Interaction Cross-Check** against your entire active profile using real-time OpenFDA data. If a conflict is detected, the system triggers a **Safety Warning**, explaining the severity and potential risks before you even take the first dose. 

For medications prescribed in India, our **CDSCO Standardization** engine automatically maps local brands to their generic equivalents, ensuring your records are accurate globally. You also get a generated **Patient-Friendly Guide** for every drug, turning complex medical inserts into simple, actionable advice."

## **Part 7: Bill Explainer (The Financial Intelligence Ledger)**
**[Visual: Opening the Bill Explainer, showing an OCR scan in progress, and the 'Dispute Letter' UI]**

**Script:**
"Medical finances are often the most stressful part of healthcare. Our **Bill Explainer** turns you into your own financial advocate. By using OCR scanning, you can upload any medical invoice, and the system will deconstruct it into a clean, **Intelligence Ledger**.

By configuring your **Insurance Reconciliation** settings—including your specific deductibles, copays, and coinsurance—Continuum simulates your exact financial liability. If the system detects pricing anomalies or double-billing based on regional benchmarks, it flags them instantly. You can then trigger the **Dispute Letter Protocol**, which generates a formal, medically-referenced letter for you to send to your provider to correct the error."
